BOARDING


Consider boarding if:


  • Your pet is social and friendly
  • Your pet has a calm temperament and is potty trained
  • Your pet is used to the home environment and has never experienced kennels or cages.

How your pet will benefit:


  • Your pet will get an opportunity to socialize with other pets and meet new people like our Pet Boarders who are loving and experienced.
  • One on One Personalised care in terms of routine feeding, exercising, and proper administration of medication if needed.
  • Trained and experienced Home pet boarders

How will you benefit:


  • You can take your long-due trip without any worries or distress.
  • You need not make frantic calls to family, friends, or neighbours, we will handle everything and take care of it.
  • You will receive frequent updates on your happy pet.
