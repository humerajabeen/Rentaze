PET TRAINING


Basic Trianing

  • Basic discipline and behavior includes:
  • Basic command,impulse control
  • leash training and toilet training.

Behavioral Training

  • Refined social behaviour(with people and other animals).
  • Includes: Reduction of excessive barking, biting,unruly chewing and breaking objects.
  • Appropriate for: Puppies and young dogs

Obedience Training

  • Obedience of commands, proofing behaviour
  • Includes All basic commands, behaviour training.
  • advanced and tricky commands to stimulate control and mental alertness.
  • Appropriate for: Young dogs of age 1-2.
